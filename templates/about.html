With the recent virus covid19 causing everyone to stay at home, either self-quarantining or social distancing, we wanted to create a way to be able to still interact and meet new people while being able to be safe at home. With the ability to swipe and find matches with new people, order food from grubhub to help local restaurants, and a video chat functionality to get to know these new people, we believe Qchat has it all in getting to interact and meet new people while being safe at home.
